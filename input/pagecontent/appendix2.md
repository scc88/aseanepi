Adopting the ASEAN FHIR ePI standard does **not** mean abandoning the familiar, local Patient Information Leaflet (PIL) formats used in your jurisdiction. 

By leveraging the FHIR ePI standard, a single structured Medicinal Product Information data file can be automatically transformed into beautiful, interactive, and locally-compliant digital leaflets.

## Core Business Benefits

1. **Write Once, Publish Anywhere:** Manage your core product data centrally. The presentation layer dynamically adapts the data to meet specific regional regulatory requirements (e.g., varying section headings).
2. **Enhanced Patient Safety:** Move beyond static PDFs. FHIR enables interactive "Clinical Details" featuring automated dosage tables, clear warning summaries, and easily accessible side-effect profiles.
3. **Cost Efficiency:** Accelerate the transition from costly printed paper inserts to dynamic, responsive digital platforms that are easier to update and maintain.
