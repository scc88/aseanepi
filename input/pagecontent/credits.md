### Credits

This Implementation Guide is published by the **Asia Partnership Conference of Pharmaceutical Associations (APAC)** to facilitate the implementation and understanding of electronic Product Information (ePI) across APAC markets.

#### Base specification

This IG is an extension of, and is designed to be consistent with, the **[Vulcan Global Core ePI Implementation Guide](https://hl7.org/fhir/uv/emedicinal-product-info/)** developed by the HL7 Biomedical Research & Regulation (BR&R) Work Group and the Vulcan Accelerator. Terminology, profiles and best-practice guidance derive from that global core.
